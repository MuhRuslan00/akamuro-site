Cookie and Tracking Technology Policy

When visiting our site, we use cookies or other technologies such as transparent GIF files, web beacons, or similar devices, including those operating in a mobile environment, to collect information that may relate to you, your preferences, or your device.

Cookies are small alphanumeric files placed on your computer. Along with similar technologies, cookies store information about your online activity, usage, and downloads on our site, as well as on the network of websites and applications where our services operate. To learn more about cookies, you can visit https://akamuro.com/.

The cookies we use include session cookies (i.e., cookies that are stored only until the end of your session on the website) and persistent cookies (i.e., cookies that remain stored even after the end of your session on the website). Cookies and similar technologies contain a unique identifier that can be used to track your activity. The information collected via cookies and similar technologies is used by us for the purposes described in our Privacy Policy. For example, we use such information to improve the user experience, analyze the performance of our site, and provide information about your use of our site to our analytics, advertising, and social media partners. Some of the information we collect through online tracking is disclosed to third parties for the purpose of targeting/retargeting advertising about our services or events.

You can go to the "Privacy Preferences" or "Privacy Settings" page to change our default settings. Please note that your settings will be lost if you visit our site from a different device, use a different browser, or reset the settings of your current browser.

Cookie Categories

Our cookies and tracking technologies are classified based on the purposes of collection and use of the information obtained as follows (note that some cookies may be used by our clients differently):

Strictly Necessary

Required for the Site to function and cannot be disabled in our systems. They are usually set only in response to your actions that constitute a request for services, such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in, or filling out forms.

Performance and Analytics

Allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our Site and Services. They help us understand which pages are the most and least popular and see how visitors move around the Site.

Functional

Help us recognize you when you revisit our site and allow us to remember your preferences (e.g., language or region selection).

Targeting

Used by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests and deliver advertisements that are more relevant to you and your interests. They are also used to limit the number of times you see an advertisement as well as to help measure the effectiveness of the advertising campaign.

Web Browser/Cookie-Based Opt-Out

You may stop or restrict the placement of cookies on your computer or remove them from your browser by adjusting your web browser preferences. There are also many programs that allow you to manually delete your cookies.

Mobile Application-Based Opt-Outs

Cookie-based opt-outs are not effective on mobile applications. Users may opt out of certain advertisements on mobile applications or reset advertising identifiers via their device settings.

  • iOS – Control Ads on Apple
  • Android – Control Ads on Google

You may also download and set your preferences on the App Choices mobile application(s) available in Google Play or the Apple App stores.

Do Not Track

There is currently no universally accepted standard for "Do Not Track" signals. Accordingly, at this time, cookies used on our website and to provide our services do not respond to "Do Not Track" signals or browser settings. You can opt out of receiving cookies by adjusting your web browser settings to reject them.
